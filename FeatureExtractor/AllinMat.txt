AllinMat is developed for environment without HDRItools to generate IMDB.
The content of it is stored in a variance called 'data'

'data' consists 40 channels:
1-3 RGB(HDR)
4-6 world position
7-9 normal vector
10-12 texture1
13-15 texture2
16 visibility
17-19 color variance
20-22 world position variance 
23-25 normal variance 
26-28 texture1 variance 
29-31 texture2 variance 
32 visibility variance 
33 x-direct gradient of world position
34 y-direct gradient of world position
35 x-direct gradient of normal
36 y-direct gradient of normal
37 x-direct gradient of texture1
38 y-direct gradient of texture1
39 x-direct gradient of texture2
40 y-direct gradient of texture2
